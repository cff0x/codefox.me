About me

I've started programming back in my childhood days out of interest in technology. Over the years, I've gained experience developing with C/C++, C#, Java, Python, Delphi and some more programming languages, as well as various databases. On top of that, I could also call myself a reverse engineer since I've been writing my own game cheats from time to time which involved dynamic and static analysis and patching of executable (mostly PE) binaries. Whenever the opportunity arises, I also develop data analysis and reporting scripts and tools for the company.

Privately, while my attention usually went into the direction of gamehacking/reverse engineering and I've also got experience in web design/web development.
